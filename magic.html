Welcome to 2025 Fall MSBA 6605 Data-Driven Analytical Models!


Instructor: Dr. Long Xia

Date: Nov. 10, 2025

Description: This course is designed to introduce students to modern data driven modeling building upon the preparatory work done in basic statistics, linear algebra, and programming. This is an introductory and overview data analytics course with a focus on understanding the conceptual foundations of data driven model building with more detailed and deeper treatment of techniques being covered in subsequent courses in the sequence.

Learning Objectives: By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • At knowledge level: you will be able to understand key data analytical models and professionally describe how they are strategically used by business in their daily operations and management.
  • At application level: you will be able to apply popular data analytical models and software (e.g., Python, Tableau) to mine actionable insights from real-world data to inform business decision-making.
  • At the design/creation level: you will be able to extensively evaluate a set of data analytical models and design/create an appropriate data analytics pipeline for business strategies.
