Welcome to 2026 Winter MSBA 6660 Deep Learning for Business!


Instructor: Dr. Long Xia

Date: Feb. 18, 2026

Description: This course will introduce advanced machine learning and deep learning with a special focus on their implications for business decision-making and strategies. We will examine how deep learning helps empower data analysis, especially unstructured data analysis, using natural language processing (NLP) and computer vision. We will cover core concepts in modern AI and deep learning by introducing various techniques, such as CNN/RNN, distributed representations, large language models (LLMs), generative models, attention and transformers, and AI Agents. Importantly, we will put deep learning, including ChatGPT and AI Agents, into the business context and explore its practical applications, particularly in the operations and supply chain analytics.

Learning Objectives: By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • At knowledge level: you will be able to understand modern AI and deep learning and describe how they are strategically applied by businesses with a special focus on natural language processing (NLP).
  • At application level: you will be able to apply deep learning-based NLP models to analyze text data in various contexts to extract useful information and insights using Python, possibly with state-of-the-art performance.
  • At the design/creation level: you will be able to evaluate the applications of deep learning-based NLP (e.g., LLMs, AI Agents) in various emerging domains and potentially create new business opportunities.
